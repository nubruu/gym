6-DAY NUBRU SPLIT

Master your physique with precision and consistency

DAY 1 – CHEST + TRICEPS (PUSH FOCUS)

Chest
  • 1 Incline Dumbbell Press 4 × 6–10
  • 2 Flat Dumbbell or Barbell Press 3 × 8–12
  • 3 Cable Fly (High → Low) 3 × 12–15
Triceps
  • 4 Rope Pushdown 3 × 10–12
  • 5 Overhead Cable Extension 3 × 12–15
  • 6 Dips (assisted if needed) 2 × AMRAP

DAY 2 – BACK + BICEPS (PULL FOCUS)

Back
  • 1 Pull-Ups or Lat Pulldown 4 × 6–10
  • 2 Chest-Supported Row 3 × 8–12
  • 3 Seated Cable Row (neutral grip) 3 × 10–12
  • 4 Face Pulls 3 × 12–15
Biceps
  • 5 Preacher Curl 3 × 10–12
  • 6 Incline Dumbbell Curl 3 × 10–12

DAY 3 – LEGS (COMPLETE DEVELOPMENT)

  • 1 Barbell Back Squat 4 × 6–10
  • 2 Leg Press 3 × 10–12
  • 3 Romanian Deadlift 3 × 8–10
  • 4 Lying or Seated Leg Curl 3 × 12–15
  • 5 Standing or Machine Calf Raise 4 × 10–15

DAY 4 – SHOULDERS + ARMS

Shoulders
  • 1 Seated Barbell or Dumbbell OHP 3 × 6–10
  • 2 Lateral Raises 4 × 12–20
  • 3 Rear Delt Cable Fly 3 × 15–20
Arms Supersets
  • 4 EZ-Bar Curl & Skull Crushers 3 × 10–12
  • 5 Hammer Curl & Cable extension 3 × 12 | 2 × 12–15

DAY 5 – UPPER BODY (HYPERTROPHY + PUMP)

  • 1 Incline Barbell Press 3 × 8–10
  • 2 One-Arm Dumbbell Row 3 × 10 each side
  • 3 Neutral-Grip Lat Pulldown 3 × 12
  • 4 Cable Fly (Low → High) 3 × 15
  • 5 Lateral Raise Dropset 2 rounds
  • 6 Face Pulls 2 × 15–20
