6-DAY NUBRU SPLIT

Master your physique with precision and consistency

DAY 1 – CHEST

  • 1 Barbell Bench Press 4 × 5–7
  • 2 Incline Dumbbell Press 3 × 8–10
  • 3 Chest Fly (Machine / DB) 3 × 12–15
  • 4 Cable Crossover (Low → High) 3 × 12–15
  • 5 Chest Dips 2 × Failure

DAY 2 – BACK

  • 1 Pull-ups / Lat Pulldown 4 × 6–10
  • 2 Barbell Row 4 × 6–8
  • 3 Seated Cable Row 3 × 10–12
  • 4 Single-Arm Lat Pulldown 3 × 12
  • 5 Face Pulls 3 × 15–20

DAY 3 – SHOULDERS + ABS

  • 1 Overhead Shoulder Press 4 × 6–8
  • 2 Dumbbell Lateral Raise 4 × 12–15
  • 3 Cable Lateral Raise (SA) 3 × 15
  • 4 Rear Delt Fly 3 × 15–20
  • 5 Hanging Leg Raise 3 × 10–15
  • 6 Cable Crunch 3 × 12–15

DAY 4 – ARMS

  • 1 EZ-Bar Curl 4 × 8–10
  • 2 Incline Dumbbell Curl 3 × 10–12
  • 3 Hammer Curl 3 × 12–15
  • 4 Cable Triceps Pushdown 4 × 10–12
  • 5 Overhead Triceps Ext. 3 × 12–15
  • 6 Dips 3 × Failure

DAY 5 – LEGS

  • 1 Barbell Squat 4 × 5–8
  • 2 Romanian Deadlift 3 × 6–8
  • 3 Leg Press 3 × 10–12
  • 4 Lying Leg Curl 3 × 10–12
  • 5 Standing Calf Raise 5 × 12–15

DAY 6 – CHEST + BACK

  • 1 Incline Dumbbell Press 3 × 10–12
  • 2 Cable Fly 3 × 15
  • 3 Wide-Grip Lat Pulldown 3 × 10–12
  • 4 One-Arm Dumbbell Row 3 × 12 / side
